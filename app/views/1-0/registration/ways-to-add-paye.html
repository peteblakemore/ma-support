Ways to add your PAYE scheme

If you know the annual payroll for your organisation is under £3 million, you can use either:

  • Government Gateway login for your PAYE scheme
  • accounts office reference number and employer PAYE scheme reference

If you know the annual payroll for your organisation is over £3 million, you can use:

  • Government Gateway login for your PAYE scheme

If you’re not sure how much your annual payroll is, choose
Government Gateway.

! Warning Before you continue, you must make sure you have authority to add PAYE schemes to the account for this organisation
