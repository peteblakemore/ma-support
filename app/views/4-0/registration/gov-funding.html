Get apprenticeship funding

If you're ready to pay for your apprenticeship training, you can do this now by adding the PAYE scheme for your organisation.

If you don't need to get apprenticeship funding yet, you can still register an account to:

  • search and save apprenticeships
  • save your chosen training providers

To get apprenticeship funding now, you can use either of the following:

  • the Government Gateway login for your PAYE scheme
  • your accounts office reference number and employer PAYE scheme reference

Can't find your Government Gateway details?

Find your accounts office and PAYE scheme reference numbers

You can find your 13-character accounts office reference number on the letter HMRC sent you when you first registered as an employer.

You will find your employer PAYE scheme reference in the payslip booklet HMRC provided. Contact HMRC or your accountant if you do not have access to the booklet.
